Clean Burning so you can light your candle responsibly



Doft candles are 100% non-toxic, cruelty-free, clean-burning candles for your heart and home. All our products are environmentally-aware, long-lasting and designed for a sustainable lifestyle.

Our handmade organic scented candles are made with a blend of organic waxes (coconut, carnauba, palm, saisol, and beeswax) and combined with natural fragrances derived from essential oils. Our captivating scents are sourced naturally and hand-created to be relaxing and energising.

The glass that surrounds the candle is 100% recyclable or reusable, while the wick can be disposed of in residual waste. The wicks used are 100% cotton and coated with a blend of special wax to give strength and high melt coating for maximum rigidity and uniform burning.
